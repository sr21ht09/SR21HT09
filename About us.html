About Us

We started in April when there was dire need of fabric masks in the market.
PrithWE provides 3-layered fabric masks in Ergonomically driven designs.

SYMBOLISM

The elephant in the logo symbolises enormity of the cause, strength for society, close bondedness for the community.

PrithWe is a blend of the words “Prithvi” (earth) and “we” for unity and social inclusivity.
